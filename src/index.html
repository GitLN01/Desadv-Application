What is a DESADV?

DESADV (DESpatch ADVice) is an electronic packing slip, which contains delivery and shipment information. A DESADV message is normally sent from the supplier to the customer, however, they can also be used with a separate logistics service provider who may then send the DESADV on behalf of the supplier.

The DESADV message typically contains the following information:

  • Identification Numbers: Unique identifiers such as Despatch Advice number, Order number, or Shipment number.
  • Dates and Times: Information about the despatch date, expected delivery date, and other relevant time-related details.
  • Parties Involved: Names and addresses of the sender, recipient, and other relevant parties involved in the shipment.
  • Product Details: Information about the goods being shipped, including product codes, descriptions, quantities, and packaging details.
  • Logistics Information: Details about the transportation method, carrier, tracking numbers, and any special handling instructions.
  • Financial Information: In some cases, the DESADV message may include invoicing or payment details.

Welcome to DESADV Data Visualization and Analysis App!

Our application offers an intuitive platform for users to seamlessly visualize and interact with inbound DESADV data related to the goods we receive. Extracted from CSV files, this data provides valuable insights that can be accessed and analyzed through our user-friendly interface. Whether you're a logistics manager, warehouse operator, or data enthusiast, our app empowers you to make informed decisions with ease.

What can you do with our app?

  • Visualize DESADV Data: Our app allows you to transform raw CSV files into meaningful visualizations. Explore your data through various chart types, graphs, and tables to gain a deeper understanding of the goods received.
  • Interact with Data: Dive into specific data points and apply filters to focus on the information that matters most to you. With interactive features, you can make data-driven decisions effortlessly.
  • Seamlessly Access Data: The app automatically integrates newly received CSV files, ensuring that you always have access to the latest information. No need for manual imports, it's all taken care of.
  • Easy-to-Use Interface: We've crafted an intuitive interface that requires no technical expertise. Simply upload your CSV files, and our app will handle the rest, guiding you through data exploration effortlessly.
  • Flexibility in Technology: As a user, you don't need to worry about the technicalities. Our app runs smoothly in a Docker container, providing a hassle-free experience.

  • Step 1: Sign in to your account or create a new one if you haven't already. Don't worry; we'll guide you through the process.
